Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the information you provide, including your name, email address, phone number, and message content. This data is transmitted through Web3Forms, a third-party form processing service.

Account Information

If you create an account on our Site, we collect your email address and authentication credentials. Account data is managed through Supabase, a third-party authentication and database service.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may automatically collect certain information, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited. This information is collected through standard server logs and Cloudflare's analytics.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our Site may use cookies set by Cloudflare for security and performance purposes. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. Google Fonts, which we use for typography, may set cookies when loading font files from Google's servers.

We use the information we collect to:

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:
